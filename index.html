Our Purpose

Ramashiya Farms exists to nurture life through responsible farming, deep respect for nature, and commitment to quality. We believe healthy animals, healthy land, and healthy communities are inseparable.

Our Vision

To become a trusted, community-rooted farm that sets the benchmark for sustainable livestock farming while contributing to food security and economic growth in South Africa.

Our Mission

To raise high-quality goats, poultry, and farm produce using ethical, transparent, and sustainable methods — delivering value to our customers while preserving the environment for future generations.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: Honest farming with transparency and accountability.
  • Sustainability: Protecting land, animals, and natural resources.
  • Quality: No compromise on animal welfare or product standards.
  • Community: Growing together with our customers and neighbours.
  • Respect for Nature: Nature is our partner, not a resource to exploit.
