Our Products

At Ramashiya Farms, we proudly breed three exceptional African goat varieties—each carrying the strength and spirit of our continent. From the resilient white Savannas to the robust Boers and the desert-adapted Red Kalaharis, every goat in our care is nurtured with traditional knowledge, modern care, and genuine love. Experience the authentic taste of Africa, raised with respect and responsibility.:

Savanna Goats

The resilient white beauties from Africa's heartland

Our pure white Savanna goats embody the spirit of Africa's vast plains. Bred with patience and nurtured with care, these gentle giants thrive under our watchful eyes, passing on their robust nature and exceptional mothering instincts through generations of careful breeding.

Boer Goats

Africa's premium meat champions

With their distinctive reddish-brown heads and sturdy frames, our Boer goats represent the perfect blend of African strength and gentle temperament. Each animal receives individual attention, ensuring they grow healthy and content in our pastures—a testament to our commitment to ethical, loving farming.

Red Kalahari Goats

Desert jewels of resilience

Bearing the beautiful red coat of the Kalahari sunset, these remarkable goats carry within them generations of adaptation to Africa's harshest lands. We honor their heritage by providing them with spacious grazing, fresh water, and the nurturing environment they deserve to flourish.

Hardbody Chickens

The Resilient African Fowls

Born from the sun-drenched landscapes of Southern Africa, our Hardbody chickens carry generations of natural resilience in their strong, compact frames. These remarkable birds—affectionately called "South African Roadrunners"—thrive with minimal intervention, embodying the self-reliant spirit of African farming. We raise them with the same care our ancestors did: allowing them to forage freely, dust-bathe in the red earth, and roost under starlit African skies. Each Hardbody in our flock receives individual attention, fresh water, and spacious roaming grounds—because happy chickens are healthy chickens, and healthy chickens are the heart of our family farm.

Hardbody Chicken Eggs

Nature's Perfect Package

Crack open one of our Hardbody eggs and taste the difference that freedom makes. Each rich, golden-yolked egg comes from hens that spend their days foraging for natural seeds, insects, and greens under the African sun. The yolks stand proud and deeply colored—a testament to their nutrient-rich diet and active lifestyle. Our family collects these eggs daily with grateful hands, ensuring they reach your kitchen with the same care and respect we show our feathered friends. Experience eggs as nature intended: wholesome, flavorful, and packed with the goodness of traditional, ethical farming.
